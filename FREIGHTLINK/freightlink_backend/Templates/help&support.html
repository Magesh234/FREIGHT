Frequently Asked Questions

To post your truck, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the "Find Available Cargo" page
  2. Click on the "Post Your Truck" button at the bottom of the page
  3. Fill out the truck details form with information about your vehicle
  4. Specify your availability dates and preferred routes
  5. Submit the form to make your truck visible to cargo owners

Your truck listing will be active for 14 days unless you extend or remove it.

FreightLink offers a secure payment system that works as follows:

  • When a match is made, the cargo owner sets up a payment in escrow
  • The funds are held securely until the delivery is confirmed
  • After successful delivery, funds are released to the truck owner
  • Our platform accepts various payment methods including M-Pesa, bank transfers, and major credit cards
  • A service fee of 5% is charged on each successful transaction

This system ensures that both parties are protected throughout the transaction process.

In case of cargo damage during transport:

  1. The cargo owner should document the damage with photos upon delivery
  2. Report the issue through the platform's "Report Problem" feature within 24 hours
  3. Our support team will review the case and contact both parties
  4. If you've opted for FreightLink's Cargo Protection, the claim will be processed according to our insurance policy
  5. Without Cargo Protection, resolution will be facilitated through our mediation process

We recommend always choosing our Cargo Protection option for valuable shipments.

To cancel a booking:

  1. Go to "My Listings" in your dashboard
  2. Select the active booking you wish to cancel
  3. Click the "Cancel Booking" button
  4. Provide a reason for cancellation
  5. Confirm your cancellation

Important: Cancellation policies vary based on how close to the pickup date you cancel:

  • More than 48 hours before pickup: No penalty
  • 24-48 hours before pickup: 25% cancellation fee
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: 50% cancellation fee

Excessive cancellations may affect your account rating.

Transport rates on FreightLink are determined by several factors:

  • Distance between pickup and delivery locations
  • Cargo weight and volume
  • Type of cargo and handling requirements
  • Truck type and capacity
  • Fuel costs and current market rates
  • Urgency of delivery

Truck owners set their own rates, but our system provides recommendations based on current market conditions. Cargo owners can negotiate rates directly with truck owners through our messaging system.
